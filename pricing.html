What's Included

What These Charges Cover

Full transparency on what each line item in your freight invoice includes.

🌊

Ocean Freight

The cost of shipping your container from major global origins to your destination — quoted in USD and converted to INR.

  • Vessel booking (ESL)
  • Port-to-port sea passage
  • Container equipment fee
  • Origin THC (at Qingdao)
  • Bunker surcharge (BAF)
⚓

Shipping Line Charges

Port and carrier charges levied at the destination in India, covering container handling, documentation, and port fees.

  • Destination THC (Mundra)
  • B/L amendment charges
  • Seal & security fees
  • Container scanning levy
  • Port congestion surcharge
📋

Forwarder D/O

Our forwarding fee for issuing the Delivery Order and coordinating with ICD Garhi for the final release of your container.

  • D/O issuance & processing
  • ICD Garhi coordination
  • Documentation audit
  • Container release support
  • GST-compliant invoice
FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Common questions about how our freight charges are structured and calculated.

Are these prices fixed or indicative?
These are sample rates based on an actual quote. Ocean freight rates fluctuate with market conditions, vessel availability, and cargo volume. Contact us for a live, binding quote.
What exchange rate is used for USD charges?
We apply a declared exchange rate (e.g., 1 USD = ₹93) at the time of invoicing. The rate is clearly stated in every quotation we provide.
Is GST included in the displayed prices?
The table shows both the base rate and the GST amount separately, so you can see a complete breakdown. Ocean freight is charged at 5% GST; Shipping Line and D/O charges are at 18% GST.
What do "Free Days" mean?
It means your container can remain at the destination port or ICD for a negotiated number of calendar days without incurring detention charges. We actively track this to avoid penalties.
Do you offer pricing for routes other than Qingdao–Garhi?
Yes. We handle multiple origin ports in China and other countries, and can route to various ICDs across India. Contact us with your specific origin and destination for a tailored quote.
Can I get pricing for LCL or 40GP containers?
Absolutely. Our pricing applies to 20GP, 40GP, and 40HC containers. LCL (Less than Container Load) pricing is also available on request and is charged on a per-CBM or per-ton basis.
