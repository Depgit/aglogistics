AG Logistics & Transport was founded with a single mission — to make international freight simple, transparent, and accessible for importers and traders across India.

5+ Years of Service
Our Story

A Company Built on
Experience & Integrity

AG Logistics & Transport was established to bridge the gap between global shipping networks and Indian importers. We noticed that businesses were often overwhelmed by the complexity and hidden costs of international freight — and we set out to change that.

From handling Ocean Freight originating in China to coordinating seamless Delivery Orders at Inland Container Depots across India, our team brings deep expertise in every aspect of the import supply chain.

We specialize in the Mundra–ICD Garhi trade corridor and work with established shipping lines to offer competitive rates, transparent GST-inclusive pricing, and dedicated after-care.
