Complete Freight Solutions

01
Ocean Freight

Global Sea Freight
at Competitive Rates

We offer Full Container Load (FCL) sea freight from major global origins — primarily China via Qingdao — to key Indian ports including Mundra. Our freight rates are quoted in USD and converted at competitive exchange rates, with full GST-compliant invoicing.

📦
FCL Container Types
20GP, 40GP, 40HC & special containers available
💱
USD Rate Transparency
Clear USD rates with declared exchange rate conversion
🛳️
Reliable Shipping Lines
ESL, MSC, COSCO & other major carriers
📅
Fixed ETD Sailing
Confirmed schedules with ETD/ETA tracking

02
Shipping Line Support

Port-Level Coordination
& Documentation

Our shipping line support service handles everything from container booking and Bill of Lading management to port-side coordination at Mundra. We ensure all documentation is compliant with both shipping line and customs requirements, and we manage free day allocations proactively.

📄
Bill of Lading Management
Original BL, Telex release & Express BL options
🏖️
14 Free Days at Destination

🔒
Compliance & Customs
Port health, customs, and carrier compliance support
📊
GST-Inclusive Charges
All shipping line charges invoiced with 18% GST

Forwarder D/O

Fast & Hassle-Free Delivery Orders

Our Forwarder Delivery Order service is designed to minimize the last-mile complexity for importers. We coordinate directly with ICDs like Garhi to ensure your containers are released promptly, with all documentation in order. Our D/O charges are among the most competitive in the market.

Competitive fixed rate + GST per container
